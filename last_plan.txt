*   **Ingredients:** 3 eggs (separated), 3 tbsp cream cheese (softened), 1/8 tsp cream of tartar, pinch of sea salt.
*   **Method:** Whisk egg whites and cream of tartar until stiff peaks form. In a separate bowl, blend egg yolks, cream cheese, and salt until smooth. 
*   **The Fold:** Gently fold the yolk mixture into the whites (don't deflate them!). 
*   **Bake:** Scoop onto a lined tray and bake at 150Â°C for 25-30 mins until golden. These are your "bread" for the bacon and eggs!
